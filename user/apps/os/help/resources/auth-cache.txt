usage: auth-cache [list|reset]
  list   - show all cached authorization decisions (default)
  reset  - clear all cached authorization decisions

When prompted for a capability authorization (disk I/O, unsigned binary),
you can answer:
  y - yes (allow this one time)
  n - no  (deny this one time)
  a - always (allow and cache the decision for future requests)

Cached decisions are per-session and persist until explicitly cleared
with 'auth-cache reset' or until the session ends.

examples:
  auth-cache
  auth-cache list
  auth-cache reset
